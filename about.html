The project

Sono Mayrit is a records and tapes collector focused on the music of the Western Mediterranean Sea, researching the connections and influences between Andalusian and North African music.

Exploring the rich Flamenco musical heritage, shaped by centuries of cultural exchange and resistance, his selections delve into the diverse soundscapes that built this dynamic art form.

Roma, Arabic and Jewish music represents the pilars of Flamenco.
